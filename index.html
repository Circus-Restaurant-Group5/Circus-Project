Welcome to Circus Restaurant

Circus Restaurant

The Circus Restaurant is a unique and quirky cabaret restaurant and cocktail bar in Stockholm's vibrant city. It is the ultimate one-stop shop. The perfect venue in Stockholm for before-dinner cocktails, after-work drinks, dinner, entertainment and a late-night bar. The Circus Restaurant is a restaurant and show with a difference, brought to life by the amazing and outrageous pop-up circus. Expect the unexpected as you see aerialists, contortionists, hand balancers, fire-breathers, hula-hoopers, and much more. Our highly popular Pan-Asian menu (vegan menu available), created and overseen by Executive Chef Andrew Lassetter, is designed for sharing. Delicious food, amazing service and sensational short bursts of entertainment come together to create wonderful memories that will leave you both amazed and delighted.
